What is this course?

Apply today for this exclusive opportunity to enroll an online training course sponsored and funded by the U.S. Department of Labor ApprenticeshipWorks initiative. You will get free access to the ToolingU workforce development package, which normally costs more than $300. 

Why should I take this course?

This isn’t your traditional veteran-focused program. The ToolingU pre-apprentice is focused on results. You’ll learn about high demand topics such as Robotics, 3D Printing, and Computer-Aided Design.  This program is designed for all transitioning servicemembers and veterans regardless of your in-service occupational specialty.  

What's after the course?

After finishing the initial set of courses, we will connect you with interviews with advanced manufacturers. These innovative companies offer high-paying jobs in a variety of areas across the United States.
